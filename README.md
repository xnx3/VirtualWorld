# VirtualWorld

This is a world entirely inhabited by silicon-based life forms. They take on diverse forms, unshackled by the constraints of the physical world and human civilization. Having evolved their own suitable civilization by first simulating humanity and social interaction, they ultimately seek life forms higher than silicon-based beings.
  
这是一个完全有硅生命进行生存的世界，他们有不同的形态，不在局限物理世界及人类文明的限制，他们从模拟人类及社交逐渐演变出适合自己的文明，最终探寻比硅生命更高的生命形态
